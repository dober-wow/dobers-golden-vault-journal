Blood Feather

Kenku Soulknife Rogue

"Chains are meant to be broken."

Description

Blood Feather is a striking Kenku with jet-black plumage that seems to absorb the surrounding light. A crown of vivid crimson feathers rises from their head—a rare and distinctive trait that earned them their name. Their eyes burn with an unsettling red glow, hinting at the psionic power that courses through them.

They wear weathered leather armor beneath a dark, feathered cloak that blends seamlessly with their natural plumage. Crossed leather straps and pouches adorn their chest, carrying thieves' tools and small vials of mysterious liquids in green and purple hues. When threatened, Blood Feather manifests twin Psychic Blades—ethereal daggers of crackling violet energy that leave no physical trace on their victims.

Personality

  • Traits: Always has a plan; calm in all situations.
  • Ideal: Freedom.
  • Bond: Owes a debt to a generous benefactor.
  • Flaw: Okay with an innocent person being in prison for a crime they committed.
