Captain Kiara Tawny

Tabaxi Battle Master Fighter

"Discipline wins wars. Instinct wins battles."

Description

Captain Kiara Tawny is a notably short Tabaxi—standing barely four feet tall—but what she lacks in height she more than makes up for in presence. Her soft, tawny-gold fur is marked with subtle tabby stripes, and her piercing green eyes carry the weight of countless campaigns, constantly scanning for threats even in moments of calm. Despite her small stature, her commanding demeanor ensures she is never overlooked.

She wears practical, battle-scarred leather armor—dark brown with a flat, military-style chest piece that prioritizes function over form. A simple brown hooded cloak is draped over her shoulders. The leather shows the marks of many battles: deep scratches, careful repairs, and weathering that speak to years of hard service.

In combat, Kiara dual-wields a pair of silver-hilted rapiers with the precision of a trained Battle Master. Her movements are fluid and cat-like, combining her natural Tabaxi agility with military discipline honed through years as a soldier.
