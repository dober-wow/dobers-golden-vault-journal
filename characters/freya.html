Freya

Eladrin Wild Magic Sorcerer / Rogue

"A fey-touched soul carrying wild magic within, balancing arcane chaos with roguish cunning."

Description

Freya is an Eladrin of striking, otherworldly beauty. Her skin is a luminous pale lavender-silver that seems to shimmer faintly like moonlight on still water. Long, flowing hair in deep violet with streaks of ethereal silver frames her sharp, fey features—high cheekbones, a narrow chin, and large almond-shaped eyes that glow with an unsettling amber-gold light, the flame-like flicker betraying the wild magic that courses through her veins.

Most striking are the faint arcane patterns that occasionally crackle across her skin like distant lightning—unpredictable surges of raw magical energy made visible. Delicate pointed ears adorned with small silver rings peek through her hair, and her expression carries a balance of mischief and mystery that keeps others guessing at her true intentions.

She wears practical dark leather armor suited for a rogue's trade—fitted but functional, with subtle buckles and pouches for thieves' tools. A hooded traveling cloak in deep purple drapes over one shoulder, ready to conceal her features when discretion is needed. At her hip hangs her Moon-Touched Sword—a shortsword that glows with pale, cold moonlight, cutting through darkness wherever she walks.
