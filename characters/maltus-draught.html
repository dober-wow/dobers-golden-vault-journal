Maltus Draught

Tiefling Trickery Cleric • Reformed Enforcer

Description

A lean tiefling with slate-grey skin and short, swept-back horns that have been filed flat at the tips—a deliberate attempt to look "less infernal" that only makes him more unsettling. His eyes are pale gold, almost milky, giving him a perpetual look of knowing more than he should. A thin scar runs from his left temple to jaw—a souvenir from a "collection" gone wrong.

Mal dresses in the understated elegance of casino floor staff: a fitted charcoal waistcoat over a deep burgundy shirt, sleeves rolled to the elbow revealing faded brand scars on his forearms—the mark of Afterlife Casino "enforcers." He carries a worn deck of Three-Dragon Ante cards that serves as his holy symbol, each card subtly defaced with symbols of the Trickster gods.

Mannerisms

Speaks in a measured, almost therapeutic cadence—the voice of someone who has talked desperate gamblers out of (and into) terrible decisions. Shuffles his cards when thinking. Never sits with his back to a door. Has a habit of naming exactly how much gold someone is carrying just by watching them move.
