Rowan

Human Gloom Stalker Ranger

"A shadow that moves before you see it. A soldier who learned to hunt what lurks in the dark."

Description

Rowan moves through the world like a held breath—silent, tense, and acutely aware. Lean and wiry, they possess the whipcord physique of someone who has spent years stalking prey through forest undergrowth and cavern shadow, where bulk is a liability and stillness is survival. Their weathered face carries the thousand-yard stare of a veteran soldier, eyes that have learned to pierce darkness and anticipate danger before it materializes.

Their dark hair is cropped short and practical, never long enough to catch on a bowstring or obscure peripheral vision. A faint network of pale scars traces their forearms—souvenirs from monstrous claws that taught hard lessons about the things that hunt in the black. They dress for function over form: well-worn studded leather armor that has been maintained with military precision, each buckle and strap positioned for silent movement and quick access.

A longbow of dark, oiled wood rests within easy reach, the weapon an extension of Rowan's patient lethality. They carry themselves with the coiled readiness of a hunting cat, weight balanced on the balls of their feet, head cocked slightly as if perpetually listening for sounds others cannot hear. In bright light, Rowan seems almost uncomfortable—too exposed, too visible. But catch them in shadow, and you'll see the transformation: a predator coming home, melting into the darkness until only their watchful eyes remain.
