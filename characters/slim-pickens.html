Slim Pickens

Lightfoot Halfling Rogue / Warlock

Description

A weathered Lightfoot Halfling with a roguish glint in his pale grey-blue eyes. His face is creased from years of squinting through smoke and shadow, with a perpetual half-smirk that suggests he knows something you don't. A thin cigarette dangles from the corner of his mouth.

He wears a wide-brimmed black hat adorned with a vulture feather tucked into its silver-buckled band, casting his features in permanent dramatic shadow. His dark leather duster is worn and travel-stained, the collar turned up against his thin, scraggly beard, and his gloved hands are never far from his belt or his blades. Worn leather cowboy boots—scuffed and dusty from countless miles—complete the gunslinger aesthetic.

The Vulture Familiar

A gaunt vulture with mottled dark plumage and piercing amber eyes that seem to hold an unsettling intelligence. Imposingly large—nearly half the height of the halfling himself when perched—its bald, wrinkled head and hunched posture give it a sinister appearance, hinting at its connection to something otherworldly—the Djinni patron that binds it to Slim.

Together

The pair move as one—the vulture perched on Slim's shoulder like a dark omen given form. Where Slim's eyes might wander or his attention drift, the vulture watches, and vice versa. They share a quiet, unspoken partnership; the halfling's smug confidence is matched by his familiar's grim patience. Together, they look like trouble waiting to happen—or trouble that's already arrived.
